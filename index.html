ABOUT AGAPE CRC

GROWING MINDS, NURTURING HEARTS, BUILDING FUTURES.

Agape Children’s Resource Center Home Project is a child focused Christian Faith based NGO working in Kibliribiri Village, Kimenyede Sub-County, Mukono District in Uganda. This project addresses social, economic, spiritual, mental, Health aspects of rural orphans and vulnerable children.

It contributes to holistic release of the potential of OVC, poverty alleviation for the OVC and caregivers, Education support and medical care, prevention and mitigation of HI V/AIDS amongst OVC.This ACRC Project will be implemented in phases. The first phase 20 OVC will be systematically selected through selection community committees, approved by the Host Church while staying with caregivers for the first 2 years. The second phase, a children’s home will be constructed with hostels and boarding facilities.
